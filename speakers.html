Speaker Profile

Frank Mwiti

CEO, Nairobi Securities Exchange

Frank Mwiti serves as the Chief Executive Officer of the Nairobi Securities Exchange (NSE), where he has been instrumental in driving technological innovation and market development across East Africa.

With over 15 years of experience in capital markets, Mr. Mwiti has led initiatives to enhance market liquidity, improve investor access, and strengthen regulatory frameworks. He holds an MBA in Finance and has been recognized for his leadership in emerging market development.

At the ASEA Conference 2025, Mr. Mwiti will share insights on leveraging technology to create more inclusive and efficient capital markets across Africa.

Session: "Digital Transformation in African Exchanges"

Date: November 26, 2025 | Time: 10:00 AM - 11:30 AM | Venue: Main Hall

Speaker Profile

Abena Amoah

Managing Director, Ghana Stock Exchange

Abena Amoah serves as the Managing Director of the Ghana Stock Exchange, bringing extensive experience in capital market operations and strategic development across West Africa.

With a distinguished career in financial services spanning over 18 years, Ms. Amoah has been pivotal in modernizing Ghana's capital markets infrastructure and enhancing investor participation. She holds advanced degrees in Finance and Economics.

At the ASEA Conference 2025, Ms. Amoah will lead discussions on sustainable finance and how African exchanges can drive ESG investment initiatives.

Session: "Sustainable Finance: ESG Integration in African Markets"

Date: November 26, 2025 | Time: 2:00 PM - 3:30 PM | Venue: Conference Room A

Speaker Profile

Jude Chiemeka

CEO, Nigerian Exchange Group

Jude Chiemeka serves as the Chief Executive Officer of the Nigerian Exchange Group (NGX), where he leads one of Africa's largest and most influential capital markets.

With over 20 years of experience in investment banking and capital markets, Mr. Chiemeka has been instrumental in implementing strategic initiatives that have enhanced market capitalization and trading volumes on the NGX. He holds an MBA and is a chartered financial analyst.

At the ASEA Conference 2025, Mr. Chiemeka will share insights on scaling African exchanges and attracting international listings to drive continental growth.

Session: "Scaling African Exchanges: Lessons from Nigeria"

Date: November 27, 2025 | Time: 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM | Venue: Main Hall

Speaker Profile

Senhaji Tarik

Vice President, ASEA

Senhaji Tarik serves as the Vice President of the African Securities Exchanges Association (ASEA), bringing extensive experience in capital market development and cross-border financial integration.

With a distinguished background in finance and economics, Mr. Tarik has been instrumental in fostering collaboration between African exchanges and promoting best practices across the continent. His expertise spans market regulation, risk management, and strategic planning.

At the ASEA Conference 2025, Mr. Tarik will lead discussions on regulatory harmonization and the future roadmap for African capital markets integration.

Session: "Regulatory Harmonization: Building Trust Across African Markets"

Date: November 26, 2025 | Time: 3:45 PM - 5:00 PM | Venue: Main Hall

Speaker Profile

Bongile Kunene

Senior Legal, Risk, Compliance Officer and Company Secretary

As an admitted Attorney, with above 9 years working experience in the legal fraternity, Ms. Kunene has diverse skills in Compliance Management, Governance, Risk Management and Anti-Money Laundering. Ms. Kunene has also worked for the Financial Services Regulatory Authority and the Ministry of Labour and Social Security, she is well vast legal experience in the administration of financial services laws and public administration laws. She is a member of the Law Society of Eswatini and the Compliance Institute of Southern Africa.

With a background in computer science and financial technology, Ms. Kunene has spearheaded the implementation of blockchain technology, automated trading systems, and mobile-based investment platforms that have enhanced market accessibility.

At the ASEA Conference 2025, she will present on the future of fintech in African capital markets and emerging technologies reshaping the investment landscape.

Session: "Digital Revolution: Technology Transforming African Exchanges"

Date: November 27, 2025 | Time: 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM | Venue: Tech Hub

Speaker Profile

Peter Nalitolele

CEO, Dar es Salaam Stock Exchange

Peter Nalitolele serves as the Chief Executive Officer of the Dar es Salaam Stock Exchange, where he has led significant market development initiatives in East Africa's capital markets sector.

With extensive experience in investment banking and capital markets, Mr. Nalitolele has been instrumental in attracting new listings and enhancing market liquidity in Tanzania. He holds advanced qualifications in finance and has contributed to regional market integration efforts.

At the ASEA Conference 2025, Mr. Nalitolele will share insights on building sustainable capital markets in emerging economies and fostering cross-border investment opportunities.

Session: "Building Sustainable Markets: The East African Experience"

Date: November 27, 2025 | Time: 11:00 AM - 12:30 PM | Venue: Regional Forum
