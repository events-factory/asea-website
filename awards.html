Inaugural Pan-Africa ESG Awards

Hosted during the 28th ASEA Annual Conference | Kigali, Rwanda | 26–28 November 2025

AFRICAN SECURITIES EXCHANGES ASSOCIATION (ASEA) IN COLLABORATION WITH ASEA ESG Awards TO HOST THE INAUGURAL PAN-AFRICA ESG AWARDS.

The African Securities Exchanges Association (ASEA) proudly announces its strategic partnership with ASEA ESG Awards, a premier provider of innovative ESG Data and Ratings solutions for Africa, to present the inaugural Pan-Africa ESG Awards during the Annual Conference of ASEA in Kigali, Rwanda from 26th – 28th November 2025.

This landmark event represents a pivotal moment in recognizing and championing ESG and sustainability excellence across the continent and globally. The awards ceremony and gala dinner will unite industry pioneers, sustainability champions, regulatory leaders, and market innovators from across Africa and beyond to honour exceptional achievements in environmental, social, and governance (ESG) practices.

An esteemed panel of adjudicators featuring ESG and capital markets specialists from Africa and internationally will meticulously evaluate award categories using evidence-based insights to guarantee transparency and impartiality. Independent validation will be conducted by ASEA's audit partners, further strengthening the credibility and trustworthiness of the evaluation process. Multiple award categories will be presented to celebrate excellence in non-financial reporting, sustainability innovation, and dedication to ethical business practices. transparency, objectivity, and credibility in our selection process. Multiple award categories will recognize excellence across ESG disclosure quality, sustainability innovation, and transformative responsible business leadership that sets new benchmarks for African capital markets.

Transparent Selection Process

The evaluation methodology is structured to enhance confidence, credibility and consistency across markets—supporting sustained sustainable value generation.

  1. Independent Assessment: Multidisciplinary committee comprising African & international ESG and capital markets authorities.
  2. Evidence-Based Evaluation: Utilizing comprehensive ESG metrics & ratings to guarantee objectivity in screening and final determination.
  3. Audit Verification: Independent validation by ASEA's audit partners to maintain integrity and procedural excellence.
  4. Comprehensive Recognition: Emphasis on reporting quality, innovation, sustainability integration & ethical conduct.
  5. Continental Influence: Driving enhanced ESG standards and investor trust across African markets.
