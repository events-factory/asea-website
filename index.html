About The Conference

Welcome to the 28th ASEA Annual Conference

This year’s proposed theme is as follows: Adapting to global market shifts and events: Strategies for resilience and growth for African Capital Markets for the future. The ASEA Conference is an annual event organized by the African Securities Exchanges Association (ASEA) in collaboration with Member Exchanges. It is a premier event that attracts participants from all over the world.

Rwanda is making the necessary preparations to host the 28th African Securities Exchanges Association Annual Conference which will be held at Kigali Serena Hotel from 26th -28th Nov 2025 in Rwanda.
