Dr. Blessing Ayemhere
Background

Dr. Blessing Ayemhere brings over 20 years of experience in the oil and gas sector, with a combined corporate experience exceeding 25 years. He has expertly managed and still manages crude oil evacuation operations.

A seasoned business strategist, financier, and sustainability leader, Dr. Ayemhere has a proven track record of delivering high-quality, efficient solutions for complex business challenges. He is a respected commentator on global business issues and challenges, leveraging his extensive market knowledge and experience in Nigeria and other countries.

Education

  • Harvard Business School Alumnus, Boston, USA (AMP – 195)
  • Chief Executive Programme (CEP 23) - Lagos Business School, Lagos, Nigeria
  • Bachelor of Science in Banking and Finance - University Of Benin, Benin City, Nigeria
Professional Qualifications
  • Fellow (FCA) - Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN)
  • Fellow (FCTI) - Chartered Institute of Taxation, Nigeria (CITN)
Key Board Positions
  • Managing Director – Umugini Pipeline Infrastructure Limited (2018 – 2024)
  • Chairman, Nigeria Ratings for ASEA ESG Awards ESG GPS (2021 - 2024)
  • INED – Saro Oil Palm Limited – Chairman, Board Finance, Audit and Governance Committee
  • Board Member - Harvard Business School Association of Nigeria (Current)
  • President: InspireXtra Empowerment Initiative - training & equipping youth for leadership and entrepreneurial success
Mr. Pierre Celestin RWABUKUMBA
Current Positions

Mr. Rwabukumba is the Chairman of the African Securities Exchanges Association (ASEA) and Chief Executive Officer of the Rwanda Stock Exchange Ltd. He is also a non-Executive Director in several institutions in finance and business development, hospitality, academia and industry in Rwanda and beyond.

Professional Background

He has over two decades of experience in financial markets services starting in New York City working in different professional positions as a stockbroker, in compliance and Operations, Institutional Investors' Sales and Advisory before joining the Central Bank of Rwanda in 2004 to help start the capital market development in Rwanda.

Key Achievements & roles

He has been instrumental in the creation of the capital market in Rwanda since inception and at the canter of the larger EAC region as Chairman of the East African Securities Exchanges Association (EASEA) and was instrumental in high level negotiations on regional integration in the East African Community and the Economic Community of Central African States (ECAS). He has served on different Capital Market Committees both at the national, regional and international levels the latest being Africa Exchanges Linkage (AELP) project where he chairs the Steering Committee, the High-Level Task Force negotiating the EAC Monetary Union and the EAC Regional Capital Markets Infrastructure (CMI) Steering Committee where he serves as Chair.

Business interests

Beyond his roles in capital markets, he serves on different boards and advisory boards within the financial services sector in Rwanda and beyond. He has also been involved in different investment projects ranging from Real Estate, Poultry and Agro – processing, manufacturing to Energy.

Education

Graduate from the State University of Buffalo (UB), New York in the United States with A bachelor's degree in economics

Alumni of a one-year international post-Graduate Advanced Management Program (AMP) for Executives run jointly by Strathmore Business School of Nairobi, the Lagos Business School, Pan- Atlantic University and IESE Business School, the University of Navara in Barcelona, Spain.

Mr. Thapelo Tsheole
Current Position

Mr. Tsheole is the Chief Executive Officer of the Capital Market Authority (CMA) Rwanda, responsible for providing strategic visionary leadership in the development, regulation and supervision of capital markets, collective investment schemes, commodity exchange and related contracts, warehouse receipts system, and other market segments.

Professional Background

He is a seasoned Financial Markets expert with over 24 years of cumulative experience, having worked for the Central Bank of Botswana and Botswana Stock Exchange (BSE). He was appointed CEO of CMA Rwanda in May 2024, having previously served as CEO of BSE from January 2016 to May 2024 and Deputy CEO since February 2014.

Education & Qualifications
  • Bachelor of Social Sciences (Single Major Economics) - University of Botswana
  • Master of Commerce (MComm) in Financial Markets - Rhodes University (RSA)
  • Master of Business Administration (MBA) - Graduate School of Business, University of Cape Town (RSA)
  • Leadership Development Programme - University of Stellenbosch Business School (RSA)
Professional Memberships
  • Member of the South African Institute of Financial Markets (SAIFM & SAFEX)
  • Member of the Institute of Directors in South Africa
Leadership Positions & Recognition
  • Former President of the African Securities Exchanges Association (ASEA) 2022-2024
  • Former Chairman of the Committee of SADC Stock Exchanges (CoSSE) 2018-2024
  • Awarded Presidential Order of Meritorious Service by the President of Botswana (September 2018)
Board Experience

Mr. Tsheole's extensive board experience includes:

  • Board Chairman of Special Economic Zone Authority (SEZA)
  • Vice Chairman of Botswana Accountancy Oversight Authority (BAOA)
  • Board member of Botswana Medicines Regulatory Authority (BOMRA)
  • Member of African Integrated Reporting Committee (AIRC) - Pan African Federation of Accountants (PAFA) and World Bank initiative
  • Current Board Member (Strategic Advisor) in The Global Principles for Sustainable Securities Lending (England & Wales)
Vision & Impact

Mr. Tsheole is a firm believer in the development of financial markets in Botswana and Africa, recognizing their importance to economic growth and stability. He has led initiatives to increase activity in financial markets and continues to drive sustainable capital market development across the continent.

Dr. Vinika D. Rao
Current Positions
  • Director, INSEAD Africa Initiative & Hoffmann Institute
  • Founding Executive Director, INSEAD Emerging Markets Institute & Gender Initiative
  • Visiting Fellow, Judge Business School, Cambridge University
Areas of Expertise

Dr. Rao's teaching and research focuses on diversity & inclusion, allyship, inter-generational influences on the future of work, and sustainability. She is frequently invited to speak on related topics and is regularly quoted and published in leading business publications.

Professional Background

She started her career with Citibank and went on to become Managing Director of an Asian conglomerate before making a serendipitous transition to education. Prior to joining INSEAD, she was Associate Professor at S.P. Jain School of Global Management across Singapore, Dubai, and Australia campuses.

Academic Experience

Dr. Rao has taught MBA, EMBA and undergraduate students and conducted specialized programmes for senior corporate executives. She manages the operations of two research centers, striving to create an active community for interaction between academia and practice.

Education & Qualifications
  • INSEAD (International Directors Programme)
  • Singapore Management University (DBA - award-winning doctorate in leadership diversity)
  • IIM Bangalore (MBA)
  • Various management programs at INSEAD and CEIBS
Board & Advisory Positions

Vinika sits on several Boards and Advisory Boards, including:

  • TiE Singapore
  • European Women on Boards
  • LiveWithAI
  • Nine by 9, Singapore
  • Kali Theatre, UK
  • Hearth Ventures, India
Global Ambassadorships & Memberships
  • Ambassador, Power Women of India
  • Ambassador, She2030 (Europe)
  • Representative, United Nations HeForShe Alliance
  • Member, Global EDI Forum, Cambridge University
  • Advisory Council, Board MatchUp (Asia)
  • International Advisory Committee, Mody University for Women
Vision & Impact

Dr. Rao believes that business has the potential to be a force for good, led by responsible managers. She is passionate about supporting under-represented group managers to make fulfilling work-life decisions, aiming to do this through active sponsorship rather than mentorship alone. She strives to engage all genders in the effort to foster DEI & ESG in their organizations and communities.
